About company

The history of eLifeLab company started in 2010 in Minsk, the capital of Belarus. For over of two years, eLifeLab has been matured into a well-organized and successful team of professionals who solve the most complex challenges for our clients in US. We've learnt to apply agile principles to deliver software of high-quality in a timely manner.

At eLifeLab, we are mostly concentrating on web technologies and possess comprehensive knowledge of web standards and tools to analyse various business opportunities in the world wide web and provide our clients with the software that corresponds to the need of their business.

Our business analysts will help you to work out the prototype of an online service, designers will make it look good and developers will do the rest to transform idea into a great product!

If you are an IT professional, eLifeLab is a great place to advance your career. We are always looking great talent, and we would love to have you join us. Thank you for considering a career at eLifeLab.
