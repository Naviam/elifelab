About company

Since 1999, members of the current eLifeLab team have been engaged in programming and design activities, having become high-skilled professionals in the fields by the year 2003. It was the year of eLifeLab foundation. Now we’re a team of new lifestyle creators, helping customers all over the world in the implementation of projects. We do not just develop web-sites or IT projects, but also help our clients to understand what they really need and get the most out of our cooperation. We believe we are responsible for our projects and provide the necessary support even after our work is done. Our mission is to out-think the competition using innovative IT ideas that are more accessible, easier to understand and easier to manage. We offer all web-design and media-design, as well as web-development services while employing various modern technologies. eLifeLab is not only keeping pace, but it also sets brand new standards in offshore IT development and IT services.
